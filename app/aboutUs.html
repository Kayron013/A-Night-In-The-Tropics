~ History ~

A Night in the Tropics began in 1972 as a local restaurant in Jamaica, W.I. called, Caribbean Cuisines. It was the love child of Thomas (Jamaican) and Petriena (Vincentian) Campbell. These two loved food, not only for the taste, but because of its unifying powers. They frequently visited other island nations and enjoyed savouring new tastes and sharing their own recipes. They would often host parties at night, where people of various backgrounds would bring a dish from their homeland and it would be a huge cultural celebration.
In 1996, after expanding to a few other Caribbean islands, they decided to bring the Tropics to the "first world", setting up locations in North and South America, and the UK. Today, A Night In The Tropics is a world reknown island experience, where people from any and every nationality can gather together and enjoy a piece of another world.
